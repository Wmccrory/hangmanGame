HALT CITIZEN


You have been judged.


Stop right there creep. This is the law. This is a society built on rules, and you have chosen not to abide by them.


These are your crimes:


  • - Possession of an unlicensed computer
  • - Unauthorized use of the intra-web
  • - Illegal possession of propaganda promoting democracy
  • - Illegal transmission of obscene photographs

You have been found guilty. The sentence? Death. However..



..You can save yourself from the judge's noose.



The Justice Department has begun a pilot program for educated criminals. Despite the seriousness of your crimes, in lieu of capital punishment you may instead choose exile into the wasteland.

You will be tasked with pacifying the mutant population near the borders of Mega-City One through rudimentary education. To be eligible for this initiative you must prove your intelligence through a test of identifying words by their length and letters. The decision is yours, but choose quickly creep, the clock is ticking...



Will you choose the gallows or the wastes?
